❗ PROBLEM STATEMENT – CROWD DENSITY MONITORING
In many public places such as railway stations, airports, malls, concerts, and religious gatherings, managing crowd density is a major challenge.
🔴 Current Problems:
Crowd monitoring is mostly manual
CCTV footage requires human operators
Humans can miss sudden overcrowding
Delayed response can lead to:
Stampedes
Accidents
Panic situations
Security risks
❌ Limitations of Existing Systems:
No real-time automated analysis
High manpower requirement
Subjective and error-prone judgment
No instant crowd classification


✅ PROBLEM YOUR PROJECT SOLVES

How can we automatically detect and classify crowd density in real time using AI?
Your system:
Automatically detects people
Counts individuals in an image or video
Classifies crowd density as:
Low
Medium
High
Works without human intervention
🧠 HOW YOUR SOLUTION HELPS
✔️ Automated crowd monitoring
✔️ Faster decision making
✔️ Reduced human effort
✔️ Improved public safety
✔️ Scalable to large areas
🌍 REAL-WORLD IMPACT
Your solution can be used by:
Police & security authorities
Event organizers
Smart city surveillance systems
Disaster management teams
It enables early warning before a situation becomes dangerous.
🎯 ONE-LINE PROBLEM STATEMENT (VERY IMPORTANT)
“Manual crowd monitoring is inefficient and unsafe; our system automates crowd density estimation using AI to enable faster and safer crowd management.”
